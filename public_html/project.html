Project

Cardessory

In this project, I worked with my Teammate, Ross Shen, to create our company, Cardessory.

Our aim for this project was to create an application which card collectors are able to keep track of their collections and stay up-to-date with the latest price and news.

Check out our live-demo of the company

Low Fidelity

Creating the low fidelity helped when we layout our project. Utilizing different coloured blocks representing our work helps give us a guide when discussing what elements fit each page.

High Fidelity

When drafting the high fidelity of the website, we were able to learn what colours worked and what to take out when discussing each element within the page.

Reflection

Overall, the project was a challenge when creating each detail. From the checkout to the contact page.

Although there were some flaws and learning what elements did not work after being graded. We learn to take away our mistakes to create better details to them.
